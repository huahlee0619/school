Overview

A Therapy Website
Built for Real Connection.

One client. One goal: replacing a scattered online presence with a warm, trustworthy, and conversion-ready therapy website.

The Client

Jared — a licensed therapist serving teens, young adults, and men looking for mental health support.

The Goal

Emotional approach + enough clear information. Build trust before the first session even begins.

Audience

Teens, young adults, men, and parents making therapy decisions for their children.

Client Brief

What Jared
Needed.

Client Requests
  • Emotional approach
  • Less text-heavy content
  • Clear focus on teens, young adults and parents
  • Easy contact process
  • Trust-building elements throughout
"Every page should make someone feel safe enough to reach out."
Research & Strategy

Designing for
Everyone.

  • 01
    Multi-generational reach

    Jared works with young children, teenagers, and their parents — each group needed a resonant experience. We designed for all three simultaneously.

  • 02
    Inclusive for all genders

    As a male therapist, Jared's brand needed to feel welcoming to both men and women. Messaging and visuals were balanced to avoid any sense of exclusion.

  • 03
    Key findings from therapy UX research

    Trust is the first conversion. Clear calls-to-action dramatically improve engagement. Branding must feel approachable, not clinical.
